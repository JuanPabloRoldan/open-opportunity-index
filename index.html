About this index

This is a verification-and-filtering layer, not another link dump. Every entry is meant to link to the official provider page, and eligibility is surfaced up front. It does not replace Fulbright, EURAXESS or DAAD — it connects them and many smaller sources through one consistent eligibility schema.

⚠ Deadlines and rules change every cycle. Treat dates here as a hint and always confirm on the official page before applying. Found something out of date? Open an issue — see “Submit an opportunity”.
